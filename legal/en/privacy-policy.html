Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how the Medi application (“the App”), available for iOS and Android devices, collects, uses, stores, and protects user-provided data. This document complies with international data protection standards, including GDPR, ARCO principles, CCPA, and App Store / Google Play best practices.

1. Data Controller

The processing of data is carried out by the development team responsible for Medi. You may contact:
soporte@nexusecosystem.com

Until the formal establishment of the commercial entity, data processing is performed by the App’s responsible developers. This does not affect user rights or the privacy measures described herein.

2. Data We Collect

Medi DOES NOT collect sensitive, medical, clinical, biometric, or financial personal data. All information remains stored locally on the user’s device.

Data stored on the device:

  • Medications registered by the user
  • Reminders and scheduled times
  • Medication intake history (local only)
  • Language, notification, and appearance preferences

Medi does not transmit, sell, or share user information with third parties.

3. App Functionality

Data is used solely to:

  • Manage personalized medication reminders
  • Organize user-entered medication information
  • Display local usage statistics
  • Improve and personalize the in-app experience

Data is not used for advertising, profiling, external analytics, or commercial purposes.

4. Storage on iOS and Android

On iOS, the App uses:

  • Secure local Sandbox storage
  • UserDefaults and iOS-encrypted files
  • Keychain when credential persistence is required

On Android, the App uses:

  • Secure SharedPreferences
  • Room Database (local only)
  • EncryptedSharedPreferences when supported by the device

No data is transmitted to external servers on any platform.

5. Permissions Requested (iOS + Android)

5.1 Notifications

Requested to enable medication reminders. Users may disable them at any time.

5.2 Camera

Used exclusively for barcode scanning. Images are neither stored nor transmitted.

5.3 Exact Alarms (Android)

Used to ensure precise reminder delivery on Android.

5.4 Background Tasks (iOS)

Allows timely delivery of scheduled notifications.

6. User Rights (ARCO / GDPR / CCPA)

Users may exercise the following rights:

  • Access — View stored information.
  • Rectification — Modify medications or settings.
  • Deletion — Delete data or uninstall the App.
  • Objection — Disable features they do not wish to use.
  • Portability — Export history as a PDF (if applicable).

To exercise any right, contact: soporte@nexusecosystem.com

7. Data Transfers

Medi does NOT share, transfer, or sell user data to third parties.

8. Security

  • File system encryption
  • Application sandboxing
  • Hardware-level restrictions
  • Background data protection

9. Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated. The most recent version will always be available at this link.

10. Contact

For questions or comments: soporte@nexusecosystem.com
